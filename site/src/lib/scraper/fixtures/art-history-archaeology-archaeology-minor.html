Archaeology Minor

  • A required 3-credit, 3xx-level course, Archaeological Methods and Practice, cross-listed as ANTH305, CLAS305, ARTH305, and JWST319Y. There is a one-course prerequisite, to be chosen from among the following: ANTH240, CLAS180, or ARTH200.
  • 3 to 6 credits in approved courses offering fieldwork experience.
  • 6 to 9 credits in supporting courses involving subject matter with a significant focus on archaeology.
