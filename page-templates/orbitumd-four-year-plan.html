OrbitUMD Four-Year Plan

Product explainer and implementation reference for the Four-Year Plan page in OrbitUMD. The page combines MAIN schedules, prior credit imports, requirement tags, and transcript-aware GPA context.

Source of truth: FourYearPlan.tsx Audience: students + maintainers Synced for current app behavior

Page Purpose

Four-Year Plan gives students a single, term-by-term timeline of completed, in-progress, and planned coursework, with summary metrics for credit progress, class standing, and UMD GPA.

Completed

Past terms plus prior/transcript credit records that count toward progress.

In Progress

The current term from MAIN schedules.

Planned

Future MAIN schedule terms.

Standing + GPA

Standing from completed credits; GPA from attempted credits and quality points.

Data Inputs

  • Saved MAIN schedules are loaded and transformed into term cards.
  • Prior credits are grouped by awarded term, including a Prior to UMD grouping when no academic term is present.
  • Requirement bundles provide tags such as Major and Minor contribution labels.
  • Course detail lookups power row-click dialogs for prerequisites and descriptions.

Credit Summary Rules

Total, Completed, In Progress, and Planned metrics are based on counted credits and intentionally exclude duplicate/repeat credits.

Metric Definition Important behavior
Total Credits Completed + In Progress + Planned counted credits Duplicate planned/in-progress repeats are excluded.
Completed Counted credits in completed terms Includes eligible prior/transcript credit imports.
In Progress Counted credits in current term Repeats of already-earned credit do not increase totals.
Planned Counted credits in future terms Repeats of already-earned credit do not increase totals.
Standing Derived from completed-credit thresholds Aligned with app-wide degree-rule interpretation.

Duplicate Credit Handling

When duplicate planned or in-progress courses repeat credit already earned, a top-page notice appears, row-level duplicate badges are shown, and those rows are excluded from counted totals.

  • Top notice summarizes duplicate count.
  • Term headers surface duplicate exclusions for each term.
  • Term cards show raw credits and counted credits for transparency.

UMD GPA Details

The page displays Overall GPA plus a Show/Hide section with Attempted Credits and Quality Points. A top-right GPA Info button explains the GPA formula and grade handling assumptions.

  • UMD GPA details table columns: Term, Attempted, Quality Points, Semester GPA, Cumulative GPA.
  • Attempted and quality-point values come from transcript/prior imports and completed-term schedule grades.
  • If no graded completed courses exist yet, a clear empty-state message appears instead of an error.

Term Layout

Each term card includes status, counted credits, duplicate flags, and disclosure controls to keep large histories manageable.

Field Meaning Notes
Course Code Canonical code or prior-credit identifier Row click opens detail dialog.
Section Schedule section code, Transcript, or Prior Credit Transcript indicates imported transcript-origin credit context.
Course Full Name Display title May be catalog, schedule, or prior-credit source text.
Credits Per-course credit value Counted totals may differ from raw totals when duplicates are excluded.
Status Completed, In Progress, or Planned Displayed with contribution tags (Major, Minor, Gen Ed) and duplicate flags.

Disclosure defaults open current and future terms and the most recent completed term; older historical sections, including Prior to UMD, remain collapsed until expanded by the user.

Accessibility And Resilience

  • Term View/Hide controls use button semantics with aria-expanded and aria-controls.
  • Course tables use semantic column headers for assistive technologies.
  • Loading, empty, and missing-data states are explicit for schedules, GPA rows, and course details.
  • The disclosure model keeps large AP/IB/prior-history datasets readable without hiding data permanently.

Sync Checklist For Maintainers

  • If duplicate-credit logic changes, update both summary metric wording and term-header exclusions.
  • If standing thresholds or policy source changes, update standing copy and info dialogs together.
  • If GPA calculations or exclusions change, update GPA Info dialog text and this reference page.
  • Keep table column labels aligned with the live Four-Year Plan UI.
